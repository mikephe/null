How do I play the game?

To play log in to your account, from your profile page select "Play" in the menu bar.
This will bring up the game page. The game will begin once the page loads.
Use the arrow keys to move the cop around game screen and use the space bar to shoot zombies.
The game is over when your player or base's health goes to 0, whichever comes first.

How do I create an account?

From the home page select Register Fill out the form by entering your first and last name a valid gamertag (3 alpha characters) and a valid passwrod (5-10 alpha/numeric characters)

How do I view my scores

To view high scores select the high scores page from the menu bar.
This will bring up a list of the top 10 high scores.
The score of your most recently played game will appear at the bottom of the page under the list of scores.
You can view other players individual scores by searching for them by their gamertag.
