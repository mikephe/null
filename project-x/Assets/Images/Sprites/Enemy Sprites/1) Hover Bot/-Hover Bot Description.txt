Enemy 1 Description:



Type:   Basic ground enemy

Health:    Moderately low (3-4 shots to kill with starting weapon or 1 charged shot)

Attacks:   Shots moderatly slow moving bullets at a low rate of fire.

Movement:    Moves slowly forward. Hovers from the ground at around the height of the player's weapon.




Role it plays in combat:

 
This is the first enemy the player will encounter in the game,
it is designed to be an easy target that is not much of a threat so that
the player can get used to the controls and combat mechanics.
