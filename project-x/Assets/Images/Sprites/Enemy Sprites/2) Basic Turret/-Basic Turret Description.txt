Basic Turret Description



Type:   Turret

Health:    Moderately Low (3-4 shots to kill with starting weapon or 1 charged shot)

Attacks:   Tracks player (if we have time) and Shoots moderatly slow moving bullets at a low rate of fire.

Movement:    Top of turret rotates to track player (if we have time) Bottom stays put. 
	     Can be placed on walls and ceilings




Role it plays in combat:

 
This is the second enemy the player enchounters. It plays the same role as the first enemy but shows that
some enemies can track player movement. Also introduces turrets as a thing in the game.
