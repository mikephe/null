TNT Bat Description:



Type:   Basic Flying Enemy

Health:    low (One shot kill)

Attacks:   Flies at player in a flock of many bats and self destructs on contact. Mid to high damage.

Movement:    Moves moderately slow. Easily shot down but makes up for it in moderately 
             overwhelming numbers (flying in a pack.)




Role it plays in combat:


This enemy is designed to teach the player to use different weapons for different situations.
The shot gun and flame thrower are highly effective at dealing with their numbers where as a single shot weapon is not.
