Poison Wheel Description:



Type:   Monster Ground Enemy

Health:    Moderate (5-6 shots)

Attacks:   Rolls toward player inflicting damage on contact. Rolls through player and turns back around 
	   to charge at player till it is destroyed (if there is time and if it fits in game play, then poison status inflicted.)

Movement:    Moves moderately fast, slow turn around though.




Role it plays in combat:


This enemy is designed to get the player used to close quarters combat and start to think of ways to deal with up close threats.
