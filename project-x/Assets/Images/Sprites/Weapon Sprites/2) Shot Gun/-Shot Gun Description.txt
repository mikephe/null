Shot Gun Description: (subject to change)



Ammo: Med (12-15 shots)

Damage Per Pellet: Low 1hp (More damage the more pellets hit)

Rate of Fire: Low - Mid (pump action between shots)

Special Trait: It's a shotgun. Shots 5 pellets (maybe more later) at once that fan out as the travel.




Role it plays in combat:


The shot gun is designed to be a great anti-air weapon and a damage dealing option for 
large enemies with more health at close range.
