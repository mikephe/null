Rail Gun Description: (subject to change)



Ammo: Low (3-4 shots)

Damage: Massive (One shot kill)

Rate of Fire: Slow (Breifly charges before firing as well)

Special Trait: Shots pierce through all enemies (but not through walls)

The Rail Gun is designed to be a heavy hitting special weapon ideal for destroying a large
